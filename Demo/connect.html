Connect

To connect a device, first click the "connect device" button. A menu will provide you with the following on-screen prompts:

  1. Ensure that the desired device is available to connect with.
  2. Select the desired device from the list of available devices.
  3. Click "Test". If the correct device is selected, it will begin to blink (indicator light reciever) or show confirmation dialogue (phone)
  4. If the correct device indicates that it has been selected, press "Confirm". If not, select and test another device from the menu until the desired device is found.
